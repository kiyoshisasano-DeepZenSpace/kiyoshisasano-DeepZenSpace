# 📘 Phase Loop Dynamics × Academic Frameworks Mapping

This document aligns key concepts from the **Phase Loop Dynamics (PLD)** framework with their corresponding terms in academic literature.  
It includes both **direct equivalences** (shared function and definition) and **analogical mappings** (conceptual or structural parallels), based on recent DeepResearch investigations.

|--------------------|---------------------------|---------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------|
