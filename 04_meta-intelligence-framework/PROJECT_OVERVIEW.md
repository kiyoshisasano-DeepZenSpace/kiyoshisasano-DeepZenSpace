### Rationale | Why Multi-Model Cognition Matters

The proliferation of LLMs has diversified cognitive capabilities. Some excel at logical consistency; others at situational nuance, multimodal synthesis, or ethical reflection. Yet everyday practice remains guided by tacit intuition:

> â€œWhy consult Model A rather than Model B?â€  
> â€œWhat division of labor justifies using several models?â€

The approach aligns with *Dual-Process Theory* *(Kahneman, 2011)*, *Systems Thinking* *(Meadows, 2008)*, *Constitutional AI* *(Bai et al., 2022)*, and the *Cognitive Division of Labor* *(March & Simon, 1958)*â€”together suggesting that intelligence emerges from the dynamic equilibrium of complementary processes.
